Welcome

Animal and plant protection association Quatro Paticas

The main objective of Quatro Paticas is to improve the living conditions of companion animals in our environment, continue as a benchmark for animal protection in Spain applying and promoting animal welfare as a work philosophy, receiving positive advice from canine educators, so that dogs and cats that pass through our Association forever recover the respect, dignity and empathy that they deserve as living beings. The great challenge is to achieve a society free of abuse and neglect. And until this is achieved, continue rescuing. Among others, our work includes:

  • Rescue and collection of abandoned dogs and cats
  • Your accommodation in our hostel or in shelter houses
  • Search for a family for each rescued animal
  • Attention and care of stable colonies of urban cats
  • Companion animal birth control campaigns
  • Prevention and reporting of animal abuse and abandonment...
